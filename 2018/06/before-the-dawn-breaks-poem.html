Before the dawn breaks

Sohani Rao | Mon 18 June 2018 | Updated on Sun 01 August 2021

Ready for the last mile,
Rising before the dawn breaks,
Putting one foot in front of another,
A light guiding our way,
Feeling the pain of the past,
And all that went wrong,
Hand in hand in hand in hand,
We march on strong,
The gates will open wide,
Our path will appear,
The sun will shine so bright,
The moon will disappear,
As we reach the summit,
The clouds bow down,
Purple velvet turns crimson,
And the future joins us,
Out of air and out of breath,
Take a moment to see,
That we are the love we seek,
And that we were always here.
