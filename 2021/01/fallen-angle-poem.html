Fallen Angel

Sohani Rao | Thu 21 January 2021 | Updated on Sun 06 March 2022

Every night I have peace,
Makes me feel guilty,
I worry about my kids,
Even when she loves me,
Moving on still hurts,
When I see your tears,
I am not the devil you see,
Just a fallen angel.

My heart knows I tried,
To not shake the den,
I sat all those nights,
Thinking about them,
You never saw me cry,
I worked it out myself,
I am just a human being,
Another fallen angel.

Get off your high ground,,
And come talk to me,
It doesn't matter how,
We ended up here,
You need to carry on,
Past your should bes,
And let me go for real,
Your fallen angel.
